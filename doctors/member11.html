Dr. Ashok Kumar Singhal
Neurologist
Dr. Ashok Kumar Singhal completed his MBBS from SP Medical College Bikaner, Rajasthan, MD in General Medicine from SMS Medical College, Jaipur; and DM in Neurology from the Prestigious Nimhans. Dr. Singhal has vast experience in the field of neurology. He has worked at Nimhans and various corporate hospitals, including Sagar Hospital and Apollo hospitals, for more than 7 years. He is especially interested in stroke (thrombolytic therapy), neuromuscular disorders, botox therapy and electrophysiology (ENMG). He has an excellent academic record and was gold medallist and Best outgoing student in MBBS. He was the recipient of the Dr. Anisiya Vasant memorial award at Nimhans in 2005, for the best resident in neurology. He is a life member of Indian academy of neurology, Indian epilepsy association and Bangalore neurological society and an associate member of international headache society.

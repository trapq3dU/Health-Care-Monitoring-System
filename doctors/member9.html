Dr. Ashlesha Bagadia
Psychiatrist
Dr. Ashlesha Bagadia is a Psychiatrist in Yelahanka, Bangalore and has an experience of 17 years in this field. Dr. Ashlesha Bagadia practices at People Tree Maarga in Yelahanka, Bangalore and People Tree Hospitals in Yeshwanthpur, Bangalore. She completed MBBS from Karnataka Institute of Medical Sciences, Hubli, (KIMS) in 2001,Fellow of the Royal Australian and New Zealand College of Psychiatrists (FRANZCP) from Royal Australian & New Zealand College Of Psyciatrists in 2013 and MRCP (UK) from Royal College of Psychiatrists, UK in 2007.

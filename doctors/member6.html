Dr. Raghavendra R
Emergency Medicine Specialists
MBBS, DNB - Anaesthesia, Fellowship in Interventional Pain Practice(FIPP),Fellowship in Pain Medicine, Diploma in Palliative Medicine Pain Management Specialist, 16 Years Experience Dr. Raghavendra R is a trained Anaesthesiologist and a consultant at Cytecare Cancer Hospital. He is a holistic physician with a focus on improving pain levels by arriving at the right diagnosis, and providing treatment that is most appropriate to the diagnosis or condition. Additionally, Dr. Raghavendra is experienced in providing related services, from basic counseling to the most advanced interventional procedure for pain management. Dr. Raghavendra has traveled abroad extensively for further training and presentations in the field, and has acquired several pain and palliative fellowships. He has conducted various educational programs and is a peer reviewer to journals. He has also been credited with several publications, and has been a guest speaker at both national and international conferences.

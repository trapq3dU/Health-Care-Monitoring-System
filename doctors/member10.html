Dr. Stalin Ramprakash
Child Specialist
Dr. Stalin Ramprakash completed his MD (Paediatrics) in Institute of Child Health and Hospital of Children, Madras Medical college in 2000. After completion of his post-graduation he had further Paediatric and Paediatric haematology oncology training in London deanery, UK. He has trained in paediatric haematology oncology in prestigious specialist London hospital such as Great Ormond Street Hospital, Royal Marsden Hospital and Royal London hospitals. He has also worked as consultant Paediatrician with haematology oncology interest for 5 years in UK before returning to India. He treating paediatric cancers, non-malignant haematological condition such as aplastic anaemia, thalassaemia, sickle cell disease and Immune thrombocytopaenias (ITP). He has performed more than 100 bone marrow transplants in children since returning to India.

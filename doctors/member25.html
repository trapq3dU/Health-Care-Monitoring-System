Dr. A Sharda
Endocrinologists
MBBS, MD - Medicine, DNB, MRCP, Fellow of American College of Endocrinology(FACE) Endocrinologist, 26 Years Experience Dr. A Sharda is a Endocrinologist and Diabetologist in Indiranagar, Bangalore and has an experience of 26 years in these fields. Dr. A Sharda practices at Samatvam Endocrinology Diabetes Center in Indiranagar, Bangalore and Vikram Hospital in Millers Road, Bangalore. She completed MBBS from The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda in 1990,MD - Medicine from The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda in 1993 and DNB from The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda in 1994. She is a member of American Association of Clinical Endocrinologists (AACE). Some of the services provided by the doctor are: Diabetes in Children and Diabetes Management etc.

MBBS, MD - Dermatology Dermatologist, 13 Years Experience Dr. Rohini Krishnamoorthy is a Dermatologist in Richmond Town, Bangalore and has an experience of 33 years in this field. Dr. Rohini Krishnamoorthy practices at Fortis la Femme in Richmond Town, Bangalore. She completed MBBS from Madras University, Chenai, India in 1985 and MD - Dermatology from The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University (TNMGRMU) in 1991. Some of the services provided by the doctor are: Dermolipectomy,Ear & Nose Piercing,Stretch Marks Treatment,Onychocryptosis and Acne/ Pimple Scars Treatment etc.

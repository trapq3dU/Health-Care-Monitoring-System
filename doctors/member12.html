Dr. Archana
Neurologist
Dr. Archana is a Consultant Neurologist at Sita Bhateja Specialty Hospital, Bangalore. Her areas of interest include Sleep Medicine, Immune-Related Neuropathies, Epilepsy and Strokes. She is also an Assistant Professor of Neurology at Bangalore Medical College. Dr. Archana has completed her MBBS from Trivandrum Medical College, MD (General Medicine) from Bangalore Medical College, and DM (Neurology) from National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences (NIMHANS), Bangalore
